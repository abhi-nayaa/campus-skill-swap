About us

What Campus Skill Swap Is All About

Campus Skill Swap is designed as a frontend landing experience for a web application that connects students who want to learn with students who are ready to teach. It helps users showcase their strengths, discover useful skills, and exchange knowledge through a simple and rewarding process.

Mission

Make peer-to-peer learning simple, practical, and accessible inside every campus community.

Vision

Create a student network where teaching and learning happen naturally beyond the classroom.

Value

Encourage collaboration, confidence, and continuous growth through shared knowledge.

Features

Built For A Better Student Experience

The platform combines discovery, skill sharing, and simple interaction flows so students can quickly find value from the moment they join.

01

Skill Listings

Students can post skills they are ready to teach and browse what others offer.

02
